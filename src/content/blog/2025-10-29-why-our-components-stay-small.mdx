Typography is the voice of design. It can whisper or shout, guide or overwhelm. At Prism Studio, we believe that great typography is what separates good websites from unforgettable ones.

## Choosing the right typeface

We approach typeface selection the way a casting director approaches a film — every font must serve the story. For Prism Studio itself, we chose Space Grotesk for headlines and Inter for body text.

Space Grotesk brings geometric precision with enough personality to feel distinctive. Inter provides exceptional readability at any size with a generous x-height and open apertures.

Together, they create a typographic hierarchy that feels both modern and approachable.

## The system behind the type

Random font sizes create visual chaos. We use a carefully tuned modular scale that ensures every heading, body text, and caption relates harmoniously to every other element.

Our scale is defined as CSS custom properties, making it trivial to maintain consistency across components and easy for editors to work within defined guardrails.

## Performance and type

Custom fonts are often the biggest performance bottleneck on a webpage. We address this with font-display swap, subsetting, and preloading critical fonts in the document head.

The goal is zero layout shift and near-instant text rendering — so the beautiful typography we design is the first thing users actually see, not the last.

## Responsive typography

Great type adapts to its container. We use fluid scaling that smoothly transitions between mobile and desktop sizes, avoiding harsh breakpoint jumps.

The result: text that always feels perfectly sized, whether on a 320px phone screen or a 2560px ultrawide display.
